## Culture

Culture refers to the shared values, beliefs, norms, and practices of a group of people that guide their behaviors, interactions, and worldview. It is a collective phenomenon learned through socialization and passed down from generation to generation

Culture is:

- Learned
- Shared
- Symbolic
- Dynamic

**Three levels of Mental Programming**

- Human Nature
- Culture
- Personality

Shared behaviours or values within a group that apply to most members, but not all.
